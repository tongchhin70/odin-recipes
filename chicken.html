Cambodian Style Fried Chicken

My favorite style of chicken, marinated in asian seasons to elevate its umami flavors.


Prep

  1. Party Chicken Wings from costco -Mix of drums and flats
  2. Remove its extra juices in the bag and dump into a ziplock bag
  3. 1tbsp of Oyster sauce
  4. 1tbsp of Fish sauce
  5. 1tbsp of Soy sauce
  6. 0.5tbsp of Chicken Boullion
  7. 0.5tbsp of Garlic powder
  8. 0.5tbsp of Sugar
  9. 1 tsp of Black Pepper powder
  10. 0.5 tsp of Salt Pepper powder
  11. Shake till everything is combined and let it marinate in the fridge for overnight

Cook

  1. Pre-heat a pot of oil to 300f
  2. Crack an egg into the ziplock bag and shake it until evenly incorporated
  3. Get a big bowl for or a large container with a lip and dump in some Mr.Hung Fried Chicken batter Mix
  4. Drop a few pieces of chicken into the container and shake until evenly coated
  5. Drop in the chicken few pieces, cook in batches to not over crowd the pan.
  6. Looking for a deep amber color with an internal temp of 165f+
  7. Lay the cooked pieces on a wire rack and serve with a bowl of rice
