Smash Burgers

This is what I had for lunch today!


Prep

  1. Half of an onion sliced very thinly
  2. Cut the lettuce - I like mine shredded
  3. Slice the tomato - If you have some
  4. Pre-heat the air fryer for 400f set for 10 mins
  5. Roll ground beef into golf ball size

Cook

  1. Add the fries to the air-fryer lil bro
  2. Get the cast iron pan ripping hot
  3. Add about a table spoon of Vegetable Oil - I used Bacon fat
  4. Add the burger ball into the pan, add the thinly sliced onions on top then press down it with a sauce pot
  5. Season it with just salt and pepper
  6. Wait for it crisp at the bottom then flip the burger
  7. Onion should steam as it cooks with the burger, then add the cheese and let it melt
  8. Prep the buns with ketchup and mayo on both buns
  9. Then add the paddy onto the bottom bum and top it lettuce and tomato, and it finish it off with the top bun
  10. Take the fries out of the air fryer and enjoy!

AND THE RESULT IS!

YOU CANNOT SKIP THE BEV, COKE OR A DIET COKE. AND ENJOY!
