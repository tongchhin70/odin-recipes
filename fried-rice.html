Mr Tong Fried Rice

Easily my favorite breakfast to make when I have left over rice


Prep

  1. Crack 2-4 eggs in a bowl then mix
  2. 1 tbsp of minced garlic
  3. 2 stalks of green onions chopped and separated
  4. Save the green tips for garnish
  5. In a small bowl add:
    • 1tbsp of Soy sauce
    • 1tbp of Sesame oil
    • 0.5tbp of Chicken Boullion
    • 1 tsp of black pepper
  1. Pre-heat a pan
  2. Add oil then cook the eggs
  3. You want big pieces of scrabble eggs not a mush
  4. Remove the egg from the pan, add more oil
  5. Fry the garlic and white parts of the green onion
  6. Before they turn golden, add in the Rice
  7. Mix then let the rice sit on the pan to crisp a little bit
  8. Add the egg and mix
  9. Add in the seasons
  10. Mix everything until fully combined
  11. Add it to a plate or bowl top with the green onion, Sesame seeds, and a touch of more of black pepper

ENJOY WITH A COFFEE OR WATER!
